About Us

Dr. Seema Dwivedi has done her Ph. D (Environmental Science) in 2002 from Chhatrapati Shahu Ji Maharaj (CSJM) University, Kanpur in collaboration with Department of Environmental Engineering, Indian Institute of Technology (IIT) Kanpur, National Botanical Research Institute (NBRI), Lucknow and National Environmental Engineering Research Institute (NEERI), Kanpur.

After her Ph. D, Dr. Seema Dwivedi finished Post Doctorate (Environmental Science) in 2009 from Centre for Rural Development & Technology (CRDT), Indian Institute of Technology (IIT) Delhi .

Dr. Seema Dwivedi is working as Associate Professor of School of Biotechnology at Gautam Buddha University

Dr. Seema Dwivedi has served as Dean of School of Biotechnology (2013-14, 2015-18) and Dean Student Affairs (2014-15) at Gautam Buddha University

Dr. Seema Dwivedi has over 15 years of experience in Teaching and Research in Environmental Sciences after completion of Ph. D. Dr. Dwivedi has expertise in Environmental Biotechnology
